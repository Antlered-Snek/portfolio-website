Art Projects

I've been doing amateur art since the start of 2020
My program of choice has always been Krita, but I occassionally use IbisPaint and FlipaClip for prototyping

  • Mechanical

    One of my biggest strengths in art that makes me stand out among other self-taught artists is my specialty in making Mechanical Designs

  • Steampunk

    One of my favorite aesthetics, I once in a while draw Steampunk as a way to gauge my overall improvement. Although I have yet to draw any Steampunk Landscape art

  • Landscapes

    I'm pretty new to making Landscape art, only really started delving into it around late 2024 while I was working for the Green WarriorZ Webinar
    My odd talent at "instilling vibes" has greatly sped my progress at making appealing and meaningful landscapes

  • Animations

    Other than creating Illustrations, I also have experience with 2D Animation
    I still use Krita for making animations, although I plan on moving to Blender for 2D and 3D Animation
    as I feel like Krita's limitations have consumed me a lot of time to work around
